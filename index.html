The Challenge

Talent Overflow, Opportunity Bottleneck

The Local Limitation

In Uzbekistan, talented IT graduates rely heavily on limited platforms like HeadHunter and LinkedIn.

Available Local Jobs ~100
Qualified Applicants ~1,000+

High competition for limited visibility.

There is a massive disconnect. Thousands of skilled graduates enter the market, but fight over a small pool of locally advertised vacancies.

Meanwhile, the global market is starving for talent, but our specialists are invisible to them, and foreign platforms are fragmented and hard to navigate manually.

  • Limited exposure to international companies.
  • Wasted potential of skilled youth.
